Leicester Hackspace was invited back to the LCB Depot for 2026’s Create-A-Con.  

Our members delivered a full day of demos and activities, showcasing a range of skills and projects,  
giving visitors a taste of what goes on at the Hackspace and what it’s all about.  

From robotics and electronics to textiles and digital making, visitors had the chance to explore, ask  
questions, see things in action and get involved in live demos and making.  

Leicester Hackspace regularly attends events, offering demos and creative activities for both children and  
adults.  

If you’re interested in having us at your event, please get in touch. Info@leicesterhackspace.org.uk
